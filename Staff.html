Where We Come From

In just two decades, the University of Johannesburg has emerged as one of Africa’s most ambitious and inclusive institutions. With growth came a deeper vision access for all, and excellence for the future.
The Development & Fundraising Office was established to help realize that vision. We champion a culture of giving that transforms today’s generosity into tomorrow’s breakthroughs advancing student success, research impact, and a stronger, more equitable South Africa. Our Work in action

We build partnerships and raise resources that fuel UJ’s mission, with a focus on:

  • Urgent student needs (food security, outstanding fees, essential supplies)
  • Transformational initiatives (scholarships, cutting-edge labs, and innovation hubs)
  • Long-term impact across Africa and beyond
  • We don’t just respond to need. We invest in possibility.
The Development and Fundraising Office holds a clear mandate: To ensure significant sustainable development through a political economic-societal impact approach, with a major aim to obtain funds for student bursaries and scholarships through purposeful collaborative partnerships.
