Alumni Fund

Give Forward, Not Back. Join fellow alumni shaping the next generation of leaders at UJ.

The University of Johannesburg takes great pride in the calibre of its alumni, who, since the institution’s inception, have continued to make meaningful contributions to the growth, development, and transformation of both the University and the broader community. The Alumni Fund builds on this legacy by inviting graduates to give back,whether through financial support, resources, or time, in order to help shape the futures of generations of students to come. Your gift, no matter the size, helps transform lives. The Alumni Annual Fund supports scholarships, research, innovation, and student support across the University. This fund gives UJ the flexibility to respond to urgent student needs,from food insecurity, to digital access, to outstanding fees. Alumni giving also fosters a culture of paying it forward and staying connected, ensuring that the impact of every contribution is felt far beyond the campus. By contributing to the Fund, alumni not only invest in their Alma Mater’s continued success but also play a vital role in strengthening opportunities for the next wave of leaders, innovators, and changemakers. Be part of our collective impact,let’s shape the next 20 years, together.
