Double our future impact

One Student. One Future. Let’s Double That.Help us match UJ’s annual R20 million investment in student registration.

Since 2009, the University of Johannesburg has committed R20 million annually to support academically deserving yet financially vulnerable students with registration fees. This investment enables more than 5 000 students each year to access higher education and begin their academic journeys. UJ recognises that many South African families face significant financial hardship, and through a range of initiatives and campaigns, the University has worked to ease the burden of tuition costs. Guided by its vision of a reimagined future, UJ remains dedicated to ensuring that all deserving students have the opportunity to pursue their education regardless of financial barriers. By joining this effort, partners and supporters not only match the University’s contribution but also amplify its impact, making it possible for even more students to realise their potential and contribute meaningfully to society.
